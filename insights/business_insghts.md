## Recommendations

- Ensure Ingredient Availability: Prioritize the supply chain for Thai Chicken, Barbecue Chicken, and Classic Deluxe ingredients, as these items represent a massive portion of total earnings.
- Optimize Dinner AOV: Launch "Evening Bundle" offers (adding appetizers or drinks) to raise the dinner AOV, which currently lags behind lunch spending.
- Strategic Staffing: Maximize kitchen and delivery staff between 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM to handle the primary volume peak and protect the highest revenue window.
- Targeted Off-Peak Promos: Introduce "Happy Hour" discounts between 2:00 PM and 4:00 PM to bridge the gap between the lunch and dinner rushes.
- Menu Pruning: Evaluate removing the bottom 5 revenue-generating pizzas to reduce inventory costs and streamline kitchen operations.
